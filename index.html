OUR STORY

How to Build a Greenhouse

Start with being in the right place at the right time. In our case, this meant being at the forefront of modern-day seniors’ housing in the late 1980’s and early 1990’s when the idea of developing accommodation that seniors wanted to live in versus what they needed to meet their healthcare requirements was really coming to fruition.

Heather Green founded Greenhouse in 1994 after having held two leadership roles in the industry. Patrick Boult and Sylvia Watts, both industry veterans, joined in 2011 and 2014, respectively; Patrick as Chief Operating Officer and partner and Sylvia as Vice President of Strategic Initiatives.

And from there we’ve built a team of like-minded individuals who love this industry and excel at their roles. What’s the common denominator? Each of us enjoys our work, likes and respects our colleagues, and feels blessed every day to serve our ultimate customer – your resident.
